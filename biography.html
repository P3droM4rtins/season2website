Biography



    Pedro Henrique Oliveira Martins, born on February 2, 1993, is a Brazilian.
Born and raised in the city of São Luís, he began his studies
in computer science in 2019, having completed Computer Networks
in Brazil.
    After working in various different sectors,
including education and finance, he discovered he was a great salesman
and worked in the field for 3 years.
    He plays a bit of the guitar and explored other activities,
such as drawing and meditation.
